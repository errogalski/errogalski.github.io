Projects

Bird Identification

Building community interest in local wildlife is a great way to protect and preserve the natural beauty of the planet. Birds are an important part of every ecosystem, and many species are rare or endangered. I built a convolutional neural network that identifies the different bird species of North America in order to make science more accessible. Using transfer learning, I was able to build a model that can identify 555 classes with an 80% accuracy. I would eventually like to pair this model with cameras at feeders and use it to identify and then notify people which birds are visiting their feeders.

Emergency Tweets

Twitter is an important way people can communicate during an emergency or disaster. However, it isn't always clear whether or not Tweets are referencing actual emergencies or are using similar language metaphorically. Using Natural Language Processing, I created an ensemble model that classified whether Tweets with certain keywords were Tweets about actual emergencies or not. The Tweets all had keywords like 'ablaze' or 'bleeding'. This project was done as a single day Hackathon and was completed in 6 hours.
